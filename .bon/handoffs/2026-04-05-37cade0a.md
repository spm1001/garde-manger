## For Claudes to come

Garde-manger's identity clarified through cross-Claude architectural dialogue. The fond architecture threatened to hollow out garde — if handoffs provide free extraction and composting moves to bon, what's left? The answer is a temporal knowledge pyramid where garde serves two distinct layers: L1 (raw full-text search over conversations — "when did we first mention X") and L5 (structured semantic extractions — builds, learnings, friction). L1 captures what Claudes didn't notice; handoffs capture what they did. Both are needed. Garde is the larder, not the kitchen — it stores what the kitchen (bon's composting) produces, and it preserves raw material (JSONL transcripts) that nobody else keeps. A tool's value can narrow without disappearing, and a clearer role is better than a sprawling one. The handoff adapter (`HandoffSource.to_extraction()`) is the interface between kitchen and cold room — the hatch through which composting's output enters garde's store.
